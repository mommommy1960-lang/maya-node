## Executive Summary

This detailed growth plan outlines the strategic initiatives, operational tactics, and measurable milestones for scaling the Maya Node ecosystem throughout 2025. Our goal is to grow from an early-stage project to a sustainable, revenue-generating platform serving 500+ organizations while maintaining our commitment to ethical AI and Commons governance principles.

**2025 Growth Targets:**
- Revenue: $270K+ (from current ~$100K projected)
- Users: 500+ organizations actively using Maya Node
- Community: 2,000+ GitHub stars, 100+ active contributors
- Team: Scale from 2.5 to 5+ FTEs
- Geographic Reach: Expand from 2 to 10+ countries

## III. Risk Management & Contingency Planning

### 3.1 Revenue Risks

**Risk: Subscription targets not met**
- **Probability:** Medium (40%)
- **Impact:** High (delays break-even by 2-3 quarters)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Diversify revenue streams (consulting, training, hardware)
  - Launch aggressive customer acquisition campaigns
  - Offer extended free trials to convert more users
  - Focus on higher-value enterprise segment
- **Contingency:**
  - Scale back team expansion (delay 1-2 hires)
  - Reduce marketing spend temporarily
  - Extend runway through bridge financing or additional grants

**Risk: Enterprise sales cycle longer than expected**
- **Probability:** High (60%)
- **Impact:** Medium (revenue delayed but not lost)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on mid-market segment (faster close, still good ACV)
  - Offer pilot programs to speed up decision-making
  - Build strong case studies from early customers
  - Bring in sales consulting for enterprise deals
- **Contingency:**
  - Increase focus on SMB segment
  - Accelerate training and consulting revenue

### 3.2 Product & Technology Risks

**Risk: Technical debt slows feature development**
- **Probability:** Medium (35%)
- **Impact:** Medium (delays roadmap by 1-2 months)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Allocate 20% of engineering time to refactoring
  - Conduct quarterly architecture reviews
  - Bring in external code review/consulting if needed
  - Prioritize scalability in Q1-Q2 development
- **Contingency:**
  - Hire additional engineering contractor
  - Postpone non-critical features to 2026

**Risk: Security vulnerability or breach**
- **Probability:** Low (15%)
- **Impact:** Critical (could halt growth, damage reputation)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Regular security audits and penetration testing
