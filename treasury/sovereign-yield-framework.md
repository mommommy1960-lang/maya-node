# Sovereign Yield Framework

## Mission
Enable autonomous, sustainable capital growth through decentralized yield optimization while maintaining sovereignty and security.

## Core Principles

### 1. Sovereignty
- Self-custody of all assets
- Non-custodial yield strategies
- Decentralized governance
- Autonomous execution

### 2. Security First
- Multi-signature controls
- Audited smart contracts
- Tiered access controls
- Emergency shutdown procedures

### 3. Sustainable Yield
- Risk-adjusted returns
- Diversified strategies
- Long-term optimization
- Downside protection

## Yield Strategies

### Conservative Tier (Low Risk)
- **Target APY:** 4-8%
- **Strategies:**
  - Staking on established networks
  - Overcollateralized lending
  - Liquidity provision on blue-chip pairs
- **Risk Level:** Low
- **Allocation:** 60% of deployable capital

### Balanced Tier (Medium Risk)
- **Target APY:** 8-15%
- **Strategies:**
  - Yield aggregators
  - Cross-chain opportunities
  - Structured products
- **Risk Level:** Medium
- **Allocation:** 30% of deployable capital

### Growth Tier (Higher Risk)
- **Target APY:** 15%+
- **Strategies:**
  - Early-stage protocols
  - Liquidity mining programs
  - Strategic partnerships
- **Risk Level:** Higher
- **Allocation:** 10% of deployable capital

## Governance & Execution

### Decision Framework
1. Strategy proposal and analysis
2. Risk assessment and modeling
3. Community review (72hr minimum)
4. Multi-sig approval (66% threshold)
5. Automated execution via smart contracts

### Performance Monitoring
- Real-time yield tracking
- Risk metric dashboards
- Automated rebalancing triggers
- Monthly strategy reviews

## Risk Management

### Protection Mechanisms
- Position size limits
- Slippage controls
- Smart contract insurance
- Emergency withdrawal protocols

### Monitoring & Alerts
- APY deviation warnings
- Impermanent loss tracking
- Smart contract event monitoring
- Market condition alerts

## Future Roadmap
- Advanced portfolio optimization algorithms
- Cross-chain yield aggregation
- Automated tax-loss harvesting
