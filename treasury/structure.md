# Treasury Structure

## Overview
The Sovereign Treasury provides autonomous capital management through decentralized asset streams and programmatic yield optimization.

## Core Components

### 1. Asset Pools
- Primary reserves
- Secondary liquidity buffers
- Strategic allocations

### 2. Capital Flow Architecture
- Inbound revenue streams
- Outbound distribution channels
- Reserve maintenance protocols

### 3. Governance Framework
- Multi-signature authorization
- Threshold-based execution
- Emergency pause mechanisms

## Design Principles
- Sovereignty: Self-custody and autonomous control
- Transparency: On-chain auditability
- Resilience: Multi-layered redundancy
- Efficiency: Automated yield optimization
